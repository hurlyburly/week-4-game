CRYSTAL COLLECTOR

Can you match the wizard and keep your collection?

The Town Wizard has challenged you to outsmart his magic crystals in order for you to win his lovely daughter, Delilah's hand in marriage.

The wizard will give you a number to match in value with a selection of magic crystals with msterious properties. Each crystal has been assigned a value that will remain hidden until you select it. Your goal is to collect as many crystals as needed to match the Wizard's number.
Be careful though, if you go over the wizard's number you lose!
